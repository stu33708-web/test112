8月25日，HASP FBA在EU/US/CA正式上线部分退款（Partial Refund）功能，该服务使卖家能够向客户提供部分退款，而无需客户实际退回商品。能够帮助卖家避免退货成本和与价值回收相关的成本，改善客户体验。 On August 25, HASP FBA officially launched the Partial Refund feature in EU/US/CA, enabling sellers to provide partial refunds to customers without requiring actual product returns. This helps sellers avoid return costs and value recovery related costs while improving customer experience.
